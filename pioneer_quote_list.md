## Home Depot Comparison Quotes (Littleton)

| Item | HD price | Project qty | HD total | vs. Pioneer |
|------|----------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Paver sand (bagged) | $5.97 / 0.5 cu ft bag | 1.1 cu yd = ~59 bags | ~$352 | Pioneer bulk ~$86 — **Pioneer wins** |
| Greystone 24"×24" paver | $10.97/each ($9.87 when >28) | ~78 units | **~$770** (at bulk rate) | Pioneer Basalite ~$2,131–$2,842 — **HD wins on price; Pioneer wins on quality/thickness (60mm vs 51mm/2in)** |
| Foam paver base panel | $11.97 / 5 sqft panel | (not recommended) | — | **Skip** — not a substitute for compacted Class 6 base |

| Pavestone 24"×16" Rect. Dark Slate (Lowe's) | 24"×16" **rect.** | $7.48/each | ~118 units | **~$882** | $2.80/sqft; 2" (51mm) thick — same as HD Greystone. Rectangle changes joint pattern vs 24"×24" plan — layout redesign needed |

> **Verdict:** Buy bulk base materials from Pioneer. HD Greystone and Lowe's Pavestone are both 2" (51mm) thick — HD is cheaper (~$770 vs ~$882) but square vs rectangle is a layout choice. Pioneer Basalite at 60mm is the only meaningfully thicker option. For a budget build, HD Greystone is the value pick; for premium, go Pioneer.

## Questions to Ask at the Counter

1. **Road base:** "Do you carry Class 6 road base, or 3/4 minus road base? What's it called here?"
2. **Delivery:** "What's the delivery minimum? Can I schedule two separate deliveries for Phase 1 and Phase 2?"
3. **Flagstone:** "Can I see samples of your flagstone? What's the price per ton for about 3 tons (~310 sqft)?"
4. **Pavers:** "Do you have the Basalite Cityscape 24×24 in stock, or do I need to order? What colors are available?"
5. **CMU blocks:** "Can I buy loose CMU blocks, or are they pallet-only?"
6. **Mulch:** "Do you carry Triple Ground Dyed Brown Hardwood in bulk? Does the brown color hold in Colorado sun? I need 11.4 cu yd."
