## Questions to Ask at the Counter

1. **Road base:** "Do you carry Class 6 road base, or 3/4 minus road base? What's it called here?"
2. **Delivery:** "What's the delivery minimum? Can I schedule two separate deliveries for Phase 1 and Phase 2?"
3. **Flagstone:** "Can I see samples of your flagstone? What's the price per ton and what's the typical waste factor?"
4. **Pavers:** "Do you have the Belgard 24×24 in gray in stock, or do I need to order?"
5. **CMU blocks:** "Can I buy loose CMU blocks, or are they pallet-only?"
6. **Mulch:** "Is the Triple Ground Black Hardwood the darkest option? Does it stay color in Colorado sun?"
