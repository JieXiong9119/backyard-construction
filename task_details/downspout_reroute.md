---

## Decision: Option B — Roll-Out Extender (chosen)

The downspout already has a roll-out extender that deploys during rain and retracts when dry. No underground work needed. The only change required is re-orienting the bottom elbow to point **east** (toward the lawn at X=192+) rather than whatever direction it currently faces, so the extender deploys across the paved patio surface toward the grass instead of into the patio zone.

This action is intentionally deferred until east-side pavers are laid, so the final elbow direction is set relative to the finished surface.

## Remaining Action — Re-Orient Bottom Elbow

**When:** After east-side pavers are laid (so the finished surface is known)
**Who:** DIY
**Time:** ~30 minutes

### Steps

1. Remove the existing bottom elbow (2–4 self-tapping screws)
2. Rotate or replace it with one pointing east toward the lawn
3. Reattach the roll-out extender — confirm it reaches past X=192 (east patio edge) when fully extended
4. If the extender is too short, replace it with a longer one (6–8 ft); search "roll-up downspout extender" or "Flex-A-Spout" at Home Depot, Lowes, or Amazon
5. Test with a garden hose — confirm discharge lands on lawn, not on pavers

### Extender length check
- Downspout at X=155, patio east edge at X=192 = ~4.6 ft
- A 6 ft extender clears the patio edge with ~1.5 ft to spare onto the lawn ✓

## Option A: Underground Burial (not chosen — kept for reference)

If the roll-out extender becomes unworkable in the future (e.g., covered by furniture, tripping hazard), the underground option remains viable.

- Trench east ~5 ft (X=155 → X=195), 12" deep
- 4" solid PVC, pop-up emitter at lawn edge
- Requires Call 811, excavation, PVC joints, gravel bedding
- Contractor cost: $200–$400

---

## Key Cautions

- **Elbow re-orientation timing:** Do this after east pavers are set, not before — the finished surface height affects how the extender lies on the patio.
- **Extender on paver surface is fine:** The roll-out extender lying flat on pavers when deployed is normal. When retracted it sits compact at the base of the downspout.
- **Foundation clearance:** Confirm the extender directs water at least 3–4 ft from the house foundation when deployed.
